Your One Stop Solution to get in touch with those who matter.

How many times have you missed an important birthday, a networking opportunity, or to get back to that person you met at that amazing conference/meet up? How long have you been procrastinating that lunch with your colleague and how long has it been since you went out on a golfing afternoon with your boss? There are many people in our personal and professional lives who play a major role in shaping us the way we are, not only to define us as how we are presently, but also to create strong future opportunities. Relationships of any nature, require effort and time, coupled with persistence. And to help you on your quest of maintaing yours, PromptMe is directed to work strictly along those lines. You can make use of this application to maintain a database of people whom you want to stay in constant touch with, set periodic intervals such that the app sends you alert texts/mails to remind you to have that lunch or go on that golf match that you have been postponing since forever.
More over, this app directs you to maintain your own personal productivity through various methods like helping you maintain notes for yourself such that you can track your state of mind throughout the time you use the app, make various to-do lists to keep track of finished and unfinished tasks as well as to assess your future goals. This app has no set way of being used, and its USP comes from what your intentions for your networking and personal productivity requirements are. So feel free to experiment with, and stay patiently tuned for all major updates that will come along the way, just for you! You can always contact the developer through the "Contact" tab. Enjoy your stay :D
