The app solves the following purposes :

1) To make a list of close people and contacts that you'd like to be in touch with
2) To mark important dates/occasions of significance like birthdays, anniversaries, custom occasions on which you'd
   like to reach out to them and be reminded for the same.
3) To set periodic reminders to get in touch with them, the time period being customizable and entirely in your hands.
4) To have a scratchpad for each user to jot down important points and be able to view them later.
5) To be able to maintain tasks and to-do lists and keep track of your personal goals


Future prospects :

1) Enable networking between close group of users
2) Enable messaging between users
